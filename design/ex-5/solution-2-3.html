Website Review Checklist

Evaluate different aspects of the website based on usability, design, and user experience.

Review Criteria

1. Fonts

  • Consistency of fonts
  • Readability of fonts
  • Aesthetics of fonts
  • Performance of fonts

2. Colors

  • Consistency of colors
  • Coherence with branding
  • Aesthetics of colors
  • Usability of colors

3. Icons

  • Clarity of icons
  • Consistency of icon styles
  • Aesthetics of icons

4. Images

  • Quality of images
  • Performance of images
  • Coherence with layout

5. Layout

  • Consistency of layout
  • Usability of layout
  • Aesthetics of layout

6. White Space Usage

  • Balance of white space
  • Aesthetics of white space

7. User Experience (UX)

  • People-centric design
  • Ease of task completion
  • Matches user expectations
